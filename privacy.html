Privacy Policy

Zen Bell respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Zen Bell is a standalone application that does not use a server or separate user accounts. We do not directly collect personally identifiable information (PII).

1. Information Collection

Zen Bell DOES NOT collect PII (e.g., name, email). However, the following information is processed for service functionality:

Type Purpose Entity
Advertising IDs (ADID/IDFA) Personalized Ads & Analytics Google AdMob
Payment Info In-App Purchase Validation Apple App Store
Local Settings App State (Ad Removal, etc.) User's Device

2. Purpose of Use and Third-Party Sharing

  • Service Maintenance: Storing subscription status locally.
  • Advertising: Providing relevant ads via Google AdMob.
  • Payment Processing: Handled securely by Apple App Store.

We do not share PII with third parties as we do not collect it. Ad and payment data are managed by Google/Apple policies.

3. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If such data is discovered, it will be deleted immediately.

4. Data Retention and Destruction

All data is stored locally on your device, not on a server. Deleting the app destroys all local information immediately.
